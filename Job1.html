Resource Planning Assistant

  • Fortna

  • 3.8
  • 39 Reviews

  • - Hanoi

Job Description:

Fortna, a Professional Consulting Company headquartered in USA, is looking for a Resource Planning Assistant for Hanoi, Vietnam office. Resource Planning Coordinator will support US Resource Planning Manager with (i.e. data entry, documentation, running reports, etc.) all that support resource planning through project online for Fortna’s Professional Services Organization.

Experience and Skills:

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

  • Supporting Resource Planning through Project Online as well as process and procedures already developed through Planning Department.

  • Reporting on key metrics (historical and forecast) to support the leadership team.

  • Working with Delivery Leadership to ensure reporting accuracy on project accounting and work force forecasting.

  • Supporting the Operations team in maintaining Contractor database and supporting onboarding/off-boarding processes.

  • Maintaining skills matrix for PSO Associates.

  • Maintaining PSO Associate resumes and process for updates.

  • Additional functions and duties as required to support PSO leadership.

  • Developing and supporting internal business systems and processes.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • 3+ years of operations support/project administration

  • MUST have strong MS Excel, MS Project & Project Online experience and analytical/research skills

  • Ability to create reports in Excel and maintain various databases

  • Doesn't mind doing data entry and highly details administrative tasks

  • Working knowledge of Project Management systems and (or) CRM systems

  • Strong problem solving and management capabilities

  • Ability to work effectively on a team and support high levels of performance

  • Ability to establish relationships and rapport with leadership

  • Ability to conduct structured problem solving

  • Ability to utilize good facilitation skills

  • Familiarity of financial analysis

  • Desire to continually add value to and ensure accuracy and quality in all work performed

  • Drive to always add value and ensure accuracy and quality in all work performed

  • Excellent collaboration, and communication skills (written and verbal communication)

  • Strong interpersonal, organizational, time management and problem solving skills

  • Ability to work United States hours (=

  • Ability to travel domestically and internationally if needed (=

  • Office location: Hanoi, Vietnam

  • Fluent in English

DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent
